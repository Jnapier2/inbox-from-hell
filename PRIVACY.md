# Privacy Notice

Last updated: July 20, 2026

**Inbox From Hell** is a static browser game. The game has no advertising, analytics, telemetry, account system, third-party login, or external API calls.

## Local data

Progress and settings are stored in the browser's local storage for the site hosting the game. That information remains on the player's device unless the player deliberately exports and shares a save file. The confirmed **Reset** action and the browser's site-data controls can clear saved progress.

## Hosting

A website or game platform may process network, device, cookie, account, payment, or usage information under its own privacy policy. That platform activity is separate from the game's local save behavior and is controlled by the platform operator.

For privacy questions, use the contact channel on the official portfolio or project page.
